Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/3 cup of mashed overripe bannanas
  • Optional nuts of the following:
    • 1/2 cup of walnuts, or
    • 1/2 cup of pecans

Kitchen Equipment

  • Oven
  • 9x5 inch loaf pan
  • Measuring cups
  • Anti-Stick Cooking Spray
  • Bowls
  • Mixer
  • Knife

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Lightly grease loaf pan.
  3. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar.
  4. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended.
  5. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture; stir just to moisten.
  6. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
  7. Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean.
  8. Let bread cool for 10 minutes in loaf pan after cooking is completed.
  9. Remove bread from pan onto a wire rack.
